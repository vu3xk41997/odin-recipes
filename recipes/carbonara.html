Carbonara

Ingredients for Two Servings

  • 2 servings of bucatini
  • olive oil
  • 6 egg yolks
  • parmigiano
  • 1 box of bacon
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the bucatini.
  2. Pan fry the bacon at the meantime.
  3. Mix the egg yolks and grinded parmigiano. Egg yolk and parmigiano should be around 1 to 1 ratio.
  4. Mix the bucatini with bacon and add some pasta water.
  5. Add the egg yolk and parmigiano and mix it though. Add more pasta water if its too dry.
  6. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
