Soy Eggs

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cooking alcohol
  • 1 cup of mirin
  • 1 cup of soy sause
  • 2 cups of water
  • 5 cloves of gerlic
  • few slices of ginger
  • 6 eggs

Steps

  1. Put everything together except eggs in a pot and boil it.
  2. Pour the sauce in a container to keep it.
  3. Boil water, add some salt, and put the eggs in when the water is boiled.
  4. Keep stirring the eggs for 6 mins so the egg yolk will be in the middle of the egg.
  5. Take out the eggs and cool them down with cold water.
  6. Peel the eggs and put them in the sauce.
  7. Keep the container in the fridge for 24 hours, flip the eggs after the first 12 hours so all the eggs will have the same color.
